Core Capabilities

Everything You Need to Stay Safe

🚨

One-Tap SOS

Instantly alert emergency contacts, nearby volunteers, and emergency services with a single tap or watch press.

📍

Live GPS + Map

Share your exact real-time coordinates and live map view. Your location updates every 5 seconds during an alert.

👥

Volunteer Network

Trained community volunteers within 1km receive instant push notifications and can respond in real time.

📞

Emergency Contacts

Save up to 5 trusted contacts. They receive SMS + push alerts with your live location link instantly.

⌚

Smartwatch Trigger

Long-press your Fire-Boltt or boAt smartwatch to silently trigger SOS — no phone needed.

🔐

Firebase Auth

Login with mobile OTP or email. Your data is encrypted, GDPR-compliant, and stored securely on Firebase.

🔔

Push Notifications

Web push notifications simulate smartwatch alerts — volunteers and contacts get notified even if app is closed.

📋

Incident History

Full log of every SOS event — time, location, who responded — stored securely in your account.

📊

Governance Dashboard

Admin panel for authorities to monitor, manage, and respond to safety incidents across the network.
